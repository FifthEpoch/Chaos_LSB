Pixel Ninja

Hide password-protected text in images

Got something to say to your bud that should never see the light of day?

Fly under the radar with Pixel Ninja, a steganography algorithm that embeds your secret in an image of your choosing. Multiple layers of security are applied to your message to ensure that even if an entity suspects there are texts hidden in the image, your message will not be compromised. Step 1: upload an image file. See recommendation and requirements. Accepted image formats:
PNG | JPEG | PDF | GIF
TIFF | SVG | BMP

Recommended minimum:
500px * 500px

Step 2: enter the message you want to hide in the image, add a password to unlock the hidden message in the future.

Step 3: click "hide message" and download the image file. Upload your image below, provide a password, hit submit to reveal hidden message.
